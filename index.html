About chromaStudios

Workspace Image

We are first-year students from ROC Nijmegen (Technovium). This game is a group project developed during the third period, from February 2 to February 12. Over the course of 8 days, we worked together to design and build this game.

The goal of the game is to solve puzzles and discover new paintings, each featuring different colors that bring the world to life.

Our team is consists of 8 students: Software Developers (Lano, Anthony, Wim, Ayham) and Media Designers (Beau, Quinten, Danique, Armina).

Game Info

Experience a narrative that flips the script—where you don't play the hero, but the one seeking forgiveness.

storyline

Play as the Bully, Not the Victim

The main character/playable character gets sent to a grey realm where she must paint reality with vibrant colors to bring life back to reality. For every single level/ place she repaints she gains a painting that regains her memories.
