About chromaStudios

Workspace Image

We are an independent game studio dedicated to crafting narrative-driven experiences that challenge perspectives and evoke deep emotions.

Our games explore difficult themes like bullying, redemption, and personal growth. We believe in creating worlds that are not just entertaining, but also thought-provoking and transformative.
